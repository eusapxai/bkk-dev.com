    BKK facilitates farmers by creating a platform where they receive advice regarding farming techniques. Many farmers do not have the required knowledge and advisory services to produce better results and do not have the means to get expert help. So we connected them to agriculture professionals who provide free service to the farmers. Further facilitating by helping them get the right price and direct access to market, modern equipment, Uberization of agricultural produce ad financial inclusion. Building a healthy relationship with the farmers, where we mutually benefit.

    As the company grows, it is further integrating with multiple businesses such as MFS (Mobile Financial Services) and insurance companies. BKK is integrated with Jazz Cash and wants to further use the MFS platforms for number of other ways. Integration with insurance companies for the provision of weather index based crop insurance, premium payments and insurance pay outs would also be facilitated by the MFS platform.

    BKK in collaboration with the Government of Sindh and KPK, has been working towards building the capacity of farmers to increase their agricultural yield and decrease cost. BKK plays a vital role in helping the government with the disaster management such as the locust outbreak, through their droneâ€™s technology. BKK aims to facilitate the government in minimizing challenges faced by farmers by providing vital data for soil monitoring and crop development, installing weather stations for better weather assessments, satellite imagery, providing on ground trainings to farmers and equipment for collaborative use.
